BRING YOUR OWN BIG WHEEL: every year on Easter Sunday San Francisco locals turn out in droves at the actual, most crooked street in the city for the annual BYOBW races. On a variety of pre-made, home-made and soon-to-be-unmade contraptions they race down in costume. There's no winner but no losers either; you won't find a single face that isn't smiling and having an incredibly good time.
